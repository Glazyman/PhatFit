How it works

From application to approval, in four steps.

1

Check eligibility

Review our eligibility guidelines. We focus on servicing orphaned children.

2

Apply Online

Fill out our online applications. The parent or guardian and the school (or other family liaison) should each submit the required forms.

3

Get Approved

Our team will review your applications and communicate with the associated school or liaison of the family.

4

Get Funded

If approved, Ahavas Chaya typically funds 75% of the therapy cost, and the family will cover the remaining amount. Learn more ›

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who's eligible for Ahavas Chaya funding?

Ahavas Chaya generally provides funding when all the below conditions are met:

  • The individual is an orphan
  • The individual is under the age of 22
  • All other funding options have been explored

For full rules, see our eligibility page ›

What mental health services do you fund?

We fund any licensed mental health professionals as long as you meet our eligibility requirements.

Will you find a professional for my child?

Typically the school, family liaison, or the family itself comes to Ahavas Chaya with a therapist, but we are always here to give recommendations when needed.

How does funding work?

Ahavas Chaya covers 75% of therapist costs and the family covers the remaining 25%. Learn more about funding ›

I have another question

Please feel free to contact our office by emailing info@ahavaschaya.com or by filling out our contact form.

Got questions? We're happy to help.

Reach out to our team — we'll respond within one business day.
