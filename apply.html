Apply for Ahavas Chaya funding today.

To apply, both the parent/guardian and the school or liaison need to complete a short form. Both forms are required.

This form should be filled out by the child's parent or legal guardian.

Before you start

Please review our eligibility guidelines first to make sure your child qualifies. Then have basic family information, school contact, and the proposed mental health provider's details ready.

If you have any questions during the process, contact us at info@ahavaschaya.com.
