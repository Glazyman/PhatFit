Managing your monthly donation

Every Stripe receipt we send (welcome email, monthly receipt, etc.) includes a one‑click "Manage subscription" link that takes you to Stripe's secure customer portal. From there you can update your card, change your monthly amount, or cancel — anytime, no account on this site needed.
